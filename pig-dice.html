Rules

Select the number of dice and the number of points you want to play to. Roll the dice as many times as you want, but at your own risk. If you roll a 1, your turn is over and you score no points that turn. If you hold before rolling a 1, you score however many points you tallied that turn. Score the number of points required for victory and win a treat!
